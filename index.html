Enterprise AI doesn't fail because of bad models. It fails because your data has no context — fragmented across Purview, Databricks, AWS Glue, Collibra, and catalogues that don't speak to each other.

Waranara is a unified metadata context layer that brings meaning to your data estate. One knowledge graph. Every catalogue. Plain English answers for your AI agents and analysts.

Named from the Dharug language of the traditional custodians of the Sydney region — waranara means to seek. That is our mission: to help your organisation find, trust, and understand its data. We pay our respects to Elders past and present.
